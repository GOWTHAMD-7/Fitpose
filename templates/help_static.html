Frequently Asked Questions
How does the AI exercise tracker work?
Our AI exercise tracker uses computer vision technology to analyze your body position and movements in real-time. It identifies key body points and calculates angles to track proper form and count repetitions accurately.
What exercises can be tracked?
Currently, our system can track push-ups, squats, shoulder-press, lateral-rise, barbell-row, tricep-dips, and alternate dumbbell curls. We're constantly adding new exercises to our tracking capabilities.
How accurate is the rep counting?
The rep counting accuracy is typically above 90% for most exercises when performed in good lighting conditions with a clear view of your body. The system is designed to only count proper form repetitions.
What are the recommended lighting conditions?
For best results, use the tracker in a well-lit room with even lighting. Avoid backlighting (such as standing in front of a window) and ensure your full body is visible to the camera.
How do I use the meal planner?
Our meal planner allows you to input your dietary preferences and fitness goals. It then generates balanced meal plans with calorie counts and nutritional information. You can customize meals and generate shopping lists.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. Your workout data and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
